## Treatment Rebuilt from Board Feedback

After the board's direction to focus more on the people doing the work rather than the technology, we rebuilt the treatment and delivered a full storyboard for review.

### What Changed

The original treatment was heavier on the technology arc — the evolution from analog to digital to NG911. The revised version shifts the weight to **the human stories** running through that arc:

- **Act 3 restructured** — now leads with location-based PSAP storytelling (not just timeline progression)
- **DHHSI line extended** — deeper exploration of the human impact
- **ECO testimonial** added — front-line communicator perspective
- **NGDispatching + PSAP interoperability** integrated into Scenario 3 as the "where we are now" anchor

### Storyboards Delivered

AI-generated storyboard panels were included to visualize key scenes and transitions. The panels cover the three-act structure with proposed shot compositions and interview framing.
