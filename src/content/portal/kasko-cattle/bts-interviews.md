## Interview Day — Round 1 Complete

June 29 was a big production day. We got everyone on camera: Ryan, Rhonda, Ella, Dylan, Parker, Jackson, Les, and Betty — all at the new Coaldale office and yard.

These behind-the-scenes photos give you a feel for the setup and the atmosphere on the day. It was a relaxed, warm environment — exactly the tone the film calls for.

The high-res originals are available in Google Drive (link above) if you'd like to download or share any of them.

## What We Captured

The interviews covered the full generational arc — from Les and Betty's perspective on where it all started, through to the next generation's vision for where Kasko is headed. That's the emotional core of the film, and it's now in the can.

**Round 2 interviews** will be scheduled for a future date to round out the story with any remaining voices or topics.
