## Garden Interview Complete

Julie Landy Deboer, president of the Coaldale Community Garden Association, sat down for an on-camera interview on **July 10** at the community garden. This was the informal field session planned to capture the community voice that rounds out the family interviews from Round 1.

### What Was Captured

- **Julie's interview** — a relaxed conversation covering the garden's origins, its connection to Kasko's new HQ site, and what community food means in Coaldale
- **B-roll** — garden beds, plantings, and the surrounding landscape while the light was good

A short b-roll sample is embedded below. The full footage will be reviewed during the edit phase.

## Where This Fits

The garden interview fills the one major gap left after the Round 1 family sessions — none of the Kasko family interviews covered the community side of the story. Julie's perspective on the garden partnership and what it means for Coaldale gives the film its "outside looking in" moment.
