## Archival Restoration — Round 1

The first round of restoration work on the Kasko family's archival photo collection is complete. These images span over a century of farming in southern Alberta — from early homestead days in the 1910s through to the modern feedlot operation.

### What You're Seeing

This gallery represents the full scope of restored archival material: family gatherings across generations, the evolution of the feedlot and farm buildings, cattle handling through the decades, and the people who built Kasko Cattle into what it is today.

Each photo has been scanned, cleaned up, and restored at high resolution. The original files are available in the Google Drive folder linked above.

### What's Next

These restored images will be woven into the Brand Origin Story as the historical backbone of the three-generation narrative. If additional family photos surface — or if there are specific images you'd like prioritized for a second restoration pass — just let us know.
