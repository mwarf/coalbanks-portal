## Icefields Parkway — Road to Rail Construction

Behind-the-scenes photos from our production shoot with Road to Rail Construction on the Icefields Parkway, near the Columbia Glacier in the Canadian Rockies. The footage captures RTRC's heavy equipment fleet — excavators, dozers, and articulated haul trucks — operating in extreme alpine conditions against one of the most dramatic backdrops in Canada.

This customer story is part of the Essex Lease 40th Anniversary documentary, showcasing how Essex's financing supports heavy construction companies working in Canada's toughest environments.

### What You're Seeing

- **Equipment in action** — Komatsu excavators, Caterpillar dozers and wheel loaders, Volvo articulated haul trucks
- **The location** — Icefields Parkway near the Columbia Icefield, Jasper National Park area
- **The conditions** — Deep snow, ice, and rock at high altitude
- **The production** — Our crew capturing cinema-grade footage with gimbal rigs, drones, and professional audio gear
