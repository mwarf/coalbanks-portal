## Project Scope

**Essex Lease Financial Corporation — 40th Anniversary Content Ecosystem**

A documentary film and photography package celebrating four decades of Essex Lease, from the 1986 founding story through to the company's position today as an independent leader in equipment financing.

The narrative arc moves from **"Grit and Growth"** — the scrappy early years — to **"Independent Fortress"** — the resilient, relationship-driven company Essex has become.

### Customer Stories

Client interviews that demonstrate Essex's flexibility compared to traditional banks:
- **Road to Rail Construction** — Essex backed them when they were just starting out
- **Boden Sand & Gravel** — a multi-generational company that's been with Essex for the long haul
- **Pitbull Energy Services** — Essex helped them navigate when oil dropped to $30/barrel
