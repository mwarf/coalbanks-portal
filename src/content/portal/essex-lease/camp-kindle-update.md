## Camp Kindle — Shane Homes Treehouse Village

On May 29, we covered Essex's participation in the Shane Homes Treehouse Village opening at Camp Kindle. Essex is a proud supporter of this initiative, and the event provided powerful community footage for the 40th anniversary film.

### What We Captured

- **Event coverage** — opening ceremony, treehouse reveal, community reactions
- **Essex equipment on-site** — exterior stills of the Essex-branded excavator used in construction
- **Community moments** — guests exploring the treehouses, outdoor activities, natural surroundings
- **Aerial footage** — drone coverage of the Treehouse Village site and surrounding forest

### Behind the Scenes

The Treehouse Village is located about 1km uphill from the main parking area — a beautiful but remote setting that made for striking footage. The treehouses are designed as accessible, inclusive spaces for campers of all abilities, and the opening day energy was genuine and emotional.

This is the kind of community story that anchors the "Independent Fortress" narrative — Essex doesn't just finance equipment, they invest in the communities where they operate.
