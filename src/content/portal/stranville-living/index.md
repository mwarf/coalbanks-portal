## Project Scope

**Better Everywhere** — a full video campaign for Stranville Living centred on the "You Deserve Better" brand promise. The campaign showcases the diversity of Stranville's builds across Southern Alberta — from urban Lethbridge to the mountain communities of Crowsnest Pass and Sparwood.

**Full package scope includes:**
- Brand anthem film
- Two documentary-style homeowner profiles
- 12–15 social media reels
- Community aerial and FPV content
