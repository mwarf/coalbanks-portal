## Abitibi Road Scout — July 13

Michael visited the two Helmsdale homes at Abitibi Road that Joel Spanos recommended from the construction team. This was an independent pre-production scout ahead of the Jul 20–23 shoot week.

### What Was Found

The site at **1255 Abitibi Road** was further along than expected — fully framed rather than at rough-in stage. The result is a more complete look at the home's bones, which shifts the visual from "construction in progress" to "the structure taking shape."

Michael adapted the plan on the fly: instead of the partially-framed architectural detail shots originally planned, the footage captures the fully framed structure with b-roll walkthroughs and drone aerials to maximize the location.

### What Was Shot

- **Ground-level b-roll** — details of framing, windows and trades.
- **Drone aerials** — the home from above, showing the roofline and placement within the neighbourhood context

### Where This Fits

This is part of the pre-production B-roll bank being built before the four-day principal shoot. The footage from Abitibi Road adds another build-stage angle to the campaign — showing the progression from framing through to finished home, which is central to the "Better Everywhere" narrative.
