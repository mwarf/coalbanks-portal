## Malloy Landing Drone Coverage — July 7–8

Michael visited Malloy Landing in Coaldale twice this week to capture independent aerial footage ahead of the production shoot. This is the same community that appears on **Day 1** of the shoot schedule (Jul 20), so getting early aerials helps plan angles and builds the asset library.

### What Was Shot

**Evening — Monday, July 7 (sunset)**
- Aerials of the Malloy Landing subdivision and surrounding area
- The **Shift Community Rec Centre** — a key landmark in the Malloy Landing community
- A partially framed Stranville townhome at framing stage — useful for showing the build process in #BetterMoments content

**Morning — Tuesday, July 8 (sunrise)**
- Returned for a second pass at sunrise to get better light direction
- Continued aerial coverage from improved sun angles (east and side-lit views)
- All drone/aerial work

### Why Two Visits

The first visit at sunset revealed that the sun direction wasn't ideal for most of the community's key angles. Rather than wait until the shoot week to find out, Michael went back at sunrise the next morning when the light was more favourable for the east-facing and side-lit coverage needed.

Malloy Landing is one of the four production locations on Day 1 (Jul 20). These early aerials mean:

- Content already banked for the #BetterMoments social reel library
