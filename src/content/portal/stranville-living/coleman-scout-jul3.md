## Coleman Scouting Trip — Friday, July 3

Michael traveled to Coleman, AB (Crowsnest Pass) to scout Stranville properties and shoot test footage ahead of the principal production window. The goal was to establish a visual vocabulary for the campaign — testing lens combinations, capturing selects, and getting aerial coverage of key locations.

### Locations Visited

| Location | Type | Coverage |
|----------|------|----------|
| **72 Kananaskis Drive** (Kananaskis Wilds) | Premium estate home | Anamorphic select reel + drone aerials |
| **Aurora Development, Phase 2** | Early construction / cul-de-sac activity | Drone aerials |
| **Hemsdale Showhome** (8619 25th Ave) | Complete, unfurnished | Interior scout |
| **Logan Duplex Showhome** (8633–8677 24th Ave) | Near complete | Exterior scout |

### What You're Seeing

Three video pieces from the scout are loaded above:

1. **72 Kananaskis Drive — Anamorphic Selects Reel** — Shot with an anamorphic lens to establish the cinematic look for high-end property coverage. This is the million-dollar home in Kananaskis Wilds.
2. **72 Kananaskis Drive — Aerial** — Drone coverage of the property and surrounding landscape.
3. **Aurora Neighbourhood Development — Aerial** — Drone footage of the Aurora Phase 2 construction area, showing early development activity.

### Why This Matters

This footage sets the creative tone for the campaign. The anamorphic look test in particular helps us align on the visual style before principal photography — locking in the aesthetic early saves time and ensures everyone's confident in the direction.
